What we do We are a solution without limits, the Cloud revolution at you doorstep. Imagine that your data is stored in a precious case, only you can access it, modify it and be sure of its location. Imagine that this case is kept inside a personal cloud in your complete control. Then imagine that this cloud can collaborate with thousands of other clouds around the world exchanging processing power with them in a completely safe manner, forming together with them the Elemento public cloud. Learn more our unique approach One ecosystem
Two blocks
Many Clouds Elemento is born to amaze! That's why we decided to divide each cloud into blocks for data (Storage Vault) and blocks for computing power (Cloud Engine) developing an efficient and intuitive proprietary operating system ElementOS to manage this perfect ecosystem. Thanks to this separation only with Elemento you will be able to take advantage of the shared power of the Elemento public cloud eliminating all the problems related to huge centralized data centers. Learn more How we do it Feel the power of choice In order to give you the best possible experience, we have tailored three ways to use the Elemento cloud according to your needs. Therefore you can:
  • Have your own cloud and absorb the costs when you don't use it entirely.
  • Own a scaled-down cloud with the ability to use the power of the Elemento public cloud based on your internal demand through a uniform hybrid cloud.
  • Rely completely on the public cloud Elemento formed by the cooperation between other private clouds by choosing to avoid any local resources except those needed for your data.
The choice is yours. Learn more
